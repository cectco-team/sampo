先刪除platform&plugins
rd /q /s platforms,plugins

加入platform
ionic cordova platform add android

build release檔
cordova build --release android

keytool -genkey -v -keystore my-release-key.keystore -alias cect -keyalg RSA -keysize 2048 -validity 10000 
若已有keystore則跳過

複製apk至與key相同目錄下

jarsigner -verbose -sigalg SHA1withRSA -digestalg SHA1 -keystore my-release-key.keystore app-release-unsigned.apk cect

cectco

zipalign -v 4 app-release-unsigned.apk HamvCect.apk
